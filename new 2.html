Welcome to Sironkoh Computer School


Sironkoh Computer School is located along the Kisumu-Ahero road in Kisumu East District of Kisumu County, approximately 11 kms from Kisumu City.Next to Rabuor Primary School and opposite Vired Showroom, Bemido building

It was established in 2007 as a private computer school by Mr Fred Akanga,BA UON. 

The Institution is hinged on the philosophy of enhancing creative, scientific, technological, innovative and critical thinking responsive to societal needs and service to humanity.

Objectives

When the school was set up in 2007, it had two major objectives:

  • To innovation and creativity in web and app development as part of a strategy to promote computer competency; and
  • To promote high quality infomation technology services: maintenance, repair, troubleshooting   and provision of computer accesories within Western Kenya.
